Coach K's Final Home Game

Final Score: UNC 94, Duke 81

  • First Half Recap
  • Second Half Recap

On a mythical night in March, UNC spoiled Coach Mike Krzyzewski's final game in Cameron Indoor Stadium. The Tar Heels came in as heavy underdogs but emerged victorious over the 4th ranked Blue Devils.

Over 90 former Duke players and numerous celebrities were in attendance. They had plans for a fun night out in Durham after what they assumed would be an easy win. Instead, it was Carolina fans who had a fun night out celebrating on Franklin St.
